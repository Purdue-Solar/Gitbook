Lessons learned:

* "In order to finish first, first you must finish"
* Simplicity wins
* Test car for 3-4 months before WSC, European teams test for even longer
* Brunnel (Nuna) and Innoptus will likely compete in ASC 2026
* Sand aeroshell to 320-400 grit, beyond is a waste of time
* Battery likely does not need cooling, but required by ASC rules (fans should not have large power draw). Battery cooling simulations are likely not necessary - **focus on manufacturing and testing**
* 6m^2 solar array is very large; Michigan has 2-part top shell hinges
* Need to debulk chassis carbon coring around hardpoints? They use Innegra
