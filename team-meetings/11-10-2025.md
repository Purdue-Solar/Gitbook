**General Engineering - BOM**

* Part naming convention in Box drive:
  * A0003\_SteeringColumnUpperAssembly
  * P0009\_UpperColumnTruss
  * [Dynamics BOM](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1mWRvkX6MqCPkF_1Sel5IMLxI-eu8ryTnW-dPTISQQUA/edit?gid=226158702#gid=226158702)

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (90).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* Why do we need a BOM?&#x20;
  * Match part number to weight and cost
* What should a BOM include?
  * Part number
  * Weight (lbs)
  * Cost ($USD)
    * Cost of COTS part, raw materials (composites), or stock
  * Unit of measure
  * Make vs. buy
* What is the BOM used for?
  * Ordering parts
  * Tracking subteam budgets so we don't go broke
  * Standardizing parts (ex. bolts)
* [Master BOM](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1TEpdzDaXZCXzlvgXYFLtNxiyENT4nE1_JZkspxLFQ24/edit?gid=0#gid=0)
